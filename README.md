# Bible Quest: Spiritual Journey Game

A gamified application designed to deepen your engagement with Scripture and prayer through consistent practice and spiritual progression.

## Features
- **Spiritual Progression:** Earn titles as you grow in wisdom and faith.
- **Mission System:** A structured path of spiritual growth with over 100+ unlockable missions.
- **Daily Engagement:** Track your Bible reading and prayer consistency.

## Mission System
The app features a linear mission system where completing one task reveals the next. Missions reward players for:
- Reading Bible passages and chapters.
- Consistent prayer at the Altar.
- Reaching new milestones in player level.
